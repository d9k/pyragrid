таблицы БД
-------------

## articles
    id, name, systemPath

## articles revisions
    id, articleId, code, datetime, userId

## goods
    id, name, price, isEgood, filePath (downloadPath)

## shopCarts
    id, createTime, total, userId, isRejectCart, parentShopCartId (for reject cart)

## shopCart_goods
    id, price, count, total, shopCartId, goodId, addTime, status

## orders
    id, shopCartId, status, shopCashFlow, isReject, userId

## orders_status
    id, orderId, status, dateTime, operatorId

## egoodsDownloadLinks
    id, domain, link, forUserId, expires

## moneyTransactions
    id, orderId, type, provider, status, shopCashflow, orderId, requestData, answerData
